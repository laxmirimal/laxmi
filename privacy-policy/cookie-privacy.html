2. Cookie Policy Explained

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help the site provide a better user experience. We use them for two main purposes: **Essential Functionality** and **Performance/Analytics**.

2.1 Essential Cookies ()

These cookies are strictly necessary for the site to perform its basic functions. They usually only store information that protects against security vulnerabilities or remembers your cookie consent preference (e.g., the laxmi_cookie_consent cookie). These do not require explicit consent.

2.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies ()

We may use third-party services, such as Google Analytics, to measure traffic and see how visitors interact with the content. This helps improve the layout and content.

Note: We only load these performance cookies after you have given your explicit consent via the cookie banner. You can clear your browser's cookies at any time to revoke consent.

3. Your Choices

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device. You can manage and delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect the full functionality of this site.
